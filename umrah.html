Steps of Umrah

1. Ihram

Make intention (Niyyah) and wear Ihram before Miqat. Recite Talbiyah.

2. Tawaf

Perform 7 rounds around the Kaaba in anti-clockwise direction.

3. Sa’i

Walk 7 times between Safa and Marwah.

4. Halq / Qasar

Shave or trim hair to complete Umrah.
