Sacred Landmarks

Explore the historical and spiritual sites of Makkah and Madinah

Cave Hira (Jabal al-Nour)

Located on the Mountain of Light, this is the blessed site where Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) received the first revelation of the Holy Quran.

Masjid An-Nabawi

The Prophet’s Mosque in Madinah. It is the second holiest site in Islam and contains the sacred Rawdah and the Prophet's (PBUH) resting place.

Mount Arafat (Jabal al-Rahmah)

The Mountain of Mercy. Standing here on the 9th of Dhul-Hijjah is the most critical pillar of the Hajj pilgrimage.
